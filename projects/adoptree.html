About the project

AdopTree is a high-fidelity mobile prototype developed to explore how a digital product can make urban tree care more accessible, motivating and easy to understand. The concept combines tree adoption, guided user flows and a future AI-based check-in feature.

My contribution

  • Full concept development
  • User flow design
  • Interface and visual design
  • High-fidelity prototyping in Figma
  • Translation of research into interaction decisions
